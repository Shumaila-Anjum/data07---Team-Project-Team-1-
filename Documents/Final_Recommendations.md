# Final Business Recommendations

## Project

StreamFlix Data Analytics Project

---

# Executive Summary

Based on the analysis of Movies, Users, and Ratings datasets, several opportunities were identified to support StreamFlix's transition to a subscription-based streaming platform.

The following recommendations are intended to improve customer engagement, subscriber growth, and overall business performance.

---

# Recommendation 1

## Invest in High-Performing Genres

Prioritise content acquisition for genres that consistently receive:

- High ratings
- Strong customer engagement
- High viewing activity

Business Benefit:

- Improved customer satisfaction
- Better return on licensing investment

---

# Recommendation 2

## Retain Top-Rated Movies

Continue licensing and promoting movies that receive the highest average ratings.

Recommended actions:

- Feature them on the homepage
- Include them in recommendation engines
- Promote them in marketing campaigns

Business Benefit:

- Increased customer retention
- Improved subscriber acquisition

---

# Recommendation 3

## Improve Personalised Recommendations

Use customer behaviour to recommend:

- Preferred genres
- Similar movies
- Trending content
- Regional content

Business Benefit:

- Increased watch time
- Better customer experience

---

# Recommendation 4

## Increase Subscriber Conversion

Develop initiatives to convert Free users into Subscribers.

Suggested initiatives:

- Free premium trial
- Exclusive subscriber-only content
- Early access to new releases
- Personalised upgrade offers

Business Benefit:

- Higher subscription revenue
- Improved customer retention

---

# Recommendation 5

## Expand into High-Engagement Markets

Use geographic insights to identify countries with strong customer engagement.

Recommended actions:

- Localise content
- Add subtitles
- Regional marketing campaigns
- Country-specific recommendations

Business Benefit:

- Increased international growth
- Higher regional engagement

---

# Recommendation 6

## Optimise Platform Performance

Prioritise development for the most commonly used devices.

Recommended focus:

- Mobile optimisation
- Desktop performance
- Smart TV experience
- Cross-device viewing

Business Benefit:

- Better user experience
- Increased customer satisfaction

---

# Recommendation 7

## Use Data for Continuous Decision-Making

Continue monitoring customer behaviour using Power BI dashboards.

Track KPIs including:

- Subscriber growth
- Average ratings
- Watch time
- Genre popularity
- Device usage
- Country performance

Business Benefit:

- Faster decision-making
- Continuous business improvement

---

# Expected Business Outcomes

Implementing these recommendations is expected to:

- Increase subscriber growth
- Improve customer satisfaction
- Increase watch time
- Improve content investment decisions
- Strengthen customer retention
- Support global expansion
- Enhance overall platform performance

---

# Conclusion

The combination of SQL, Python, and Power BI provides StreamFlix with a scalable analytics solution capable of supporting future business growth.

The interactive dashboard enables stakeholders to monitor customer behaviour, evaluate content performance, and make informed strategic decisions based on reliable data.
